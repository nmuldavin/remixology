Remixology

Remixology is a web-app for organizing and comparing different versions of cocktail recipes. For example, you might be an avid home mixologist interested in logging and comparing your experimentations as you improve your recipes. You might want to compare two or more Old Fashioned recipes you found online or in books. You might be an avid cocktail enthusiast interested in logging the tips and tricks you hear from your favorite bartenders.

To see how it works, check out this example.

Remixology is a work in progress. Be aware that currently all content is viewable by the general public. Your password is secure but your content is not at this point.
